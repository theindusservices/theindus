Indus services was founded by Mr. Ved Prakash Sharma on 27th November 1999 as a sole proprietor. Since then the organization is leading a path of delivering quality and commitment to its prestigious clients.

Mr. Ved Prakash Sharma with experience of more than 25 years in Designing and Execution of building services like Air-conditioning, Fire-fighting, sanitation, plumbing and building automation systems.
